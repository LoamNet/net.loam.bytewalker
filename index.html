Bytewalker

What is Bytewalker?

Bytewalker is a untity game that's current under development, and near the end of alpha! It's a 2D tile-based strategy game about digital warfare in a far future environment. The game is free-to-play and always will be, funded only by donations and ads that don't interfere with gameplay.

The title is experimental, and development is being supported by Loam! As far as people who've contributed, the project was created by Wisp and some of the art and models were made by Kaide!

What's the inspiration?

A long time ago, a game called Spybotics: Nightfall Incident was created for LEGO. I'm not sure how successful the product line was itself, but the game and theme were one of the most interesting tile based strategy games I could find at the time, and they kicked off my interest in games like Fire Emblem. I wanted to explore some of the elements that made the game fun for me, and so Bytewalker was created! (or at least is being created!)
